Services


Individual Psychotherapy:

We offer collaborative sessions with a therapist to address personal challenges, improve mental well-being, and promote personal growth through confidential and tailored sessions.

Marital/Family Therapy

We provide therapeutic support for improving relational dynamics and communication within family units.

Psychological Assessment:

We use standardised tests, interviews, and observations to gather information about an individual's cognitive, emotional, social, and behavioral functioning. We offer Cognitive Assessment and Personality Assessment


Psychotherapy is ultimately about deepening our self-knowledge as we work towards understanding the meaning we have made of our past and how this may continue to impact our experience of the present. Whilst this is a challenging process, it can lead to a greater sense of autonomy and trust in our ability to create a life of our own choosing.

Practice areas include the following:

  • Issues of self-esteem and identity.
  • Life crises.
  • Adjustment difficulties.
  • Relationship issues.
  • Anxiety and phobias.
  • Depression and bipolar disorders.
  • Obsessive compulsive disorders.
  • Sexual/emotional/physical abuse.
  • Personality disorders.
  • Acute stress.
  • Suicidality and self-harm.
  • Eating disorders.
  • Stress and Burnout.
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder.
