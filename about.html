About me

I am a registered HPCSA Clinical Psychologist located in Randburg and Braamfontein. My practices encompass a wide array of offerings, including individual, couples, and family therapy. I have an interest in working with children, teenagers, and adults with a wide range of emotional and psychological difficulties. Using an integrative approach to cater for every client’s needs. l completed a yearlong internship at a Dr George Mukhari Academic hospital where l worked at Clinical Psychology Unit, Psychiatric Unit and Medical unit.. I completed my community service year at a correctional facility where I provided mental health services to a broad spectrum of adult inmates. I strive to create an open and unconditionally accepting therapeutic environment in which you feel safe to explore your problems and concerns. I have much appreciation for the uniqueness of each individual and respect your innate ability to generate insight.
